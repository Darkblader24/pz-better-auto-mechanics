description=[list]
description=[*] [b]0 (Risky):[/b] All parts will be worked on. Be careful‚Äîparts [i]will[/i] break or lose condition at lower skill levels.
description=[*] [b]30 (Default):[/b] A good balance. Parts with a very low success chance are skipped to prevent excessive damage, while still allowing for decent XP gain.
