description=[h2]üîß Key Features[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]Smart XP Tracking:[/b] The mod knows exactly when you can't gain XP from a specific car anymore. It will alert you if a vehicle isn't worth training on, so you never waste time.
description=[*] [b]Resume Interrupted Training:[/b] If zombies interrupt you (or you cancel an action), you can pick up exactly where you left off. No more starting from scratch and wasting part durability!
description=[*] [b]Efficiency First:[/b] The mod will never perform an action if it won't yield XP.
description=[*] [b]Clearer Tooltips:[/b] Know exactly what tools you need and how your level and recipes impact the training before you start.
description=[*] [b]Less Confusing:[/b] This mod has a single and clearly explained option for the "Minimum Part Success Chance". This setting can be overwritten by server owners in the sandbox settings and is clearly explained in the button tooltip if thats the case.
description=[*] [b]Lightweight:[/b] Its lightweight code base makes it less likely to break with future game updates
description=[*] [b]Translated into:[/b] English, German, Russian (credits to [url=https://steamcommunity.com/id/Templarum]Kavinsky[/url]!), French, simple + trad. Chinese, Japanese, and Spanish
